What is an Art Gallery?

An art gallery is a space where visual art is displayed, preserved, and shared with the public.

  • Exhibition spaces for artists to showcase their work
  • Commercial venues for buying and selling art
  • Cultural hubs for community engagement
  • Educational centers for art appreciation
  • Preservation spaces for cultural heritage
